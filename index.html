WACV 2026 Accepted

FSP-DETR: Few-shot Prototypical Parasitic Ova Detection

Shubham Trehan, Udhav Ramachandran, Akash Rao, Ruth Scimeca, S.N Aakur

Developed a unified transformer-based framework for few-shot and zero-shot object detection in biomedical images. The approach uses prototype-driven proposals combined with supervised embedding strategies, enabling robust detection under extreme label scarcity. Significantly outperformed existing methods on novel parasite ova and malaria benchmarks.
